Our Services

A Full Service Leasing Company

Beacon Intermodal Leasing, LLC (“Beacon”) is a full-service container leasing company serving the international shipping industry. Beacon's flexibility to meet the specific requirements of our customers includes a wide range of available leasing options, container resale and asset management. We back up our service with a global network of offices and agents. Beacon's ongoing procurement program ensures continual availability of new, high quality equipment in demand locations at the right time. Our team of dedicated professionals will work with you to meet your financial and operational needs. With an average of 22 years’ container industry experience, we can offer unparalleled service with a commitment to quality.

Our customers can rely on Beacon for equipment where and when it is needed with leases custom-tailored to meet their strategic, operational and financial requirements.

Leasing

Beacon specializes in offering flexible leasing options tailored to the needs of our clients

Container Resales

Beacon sells new and used containers to a variety of customers in different industries

Asset Management

Beacon actively manages container portfolios on behalf of third party investors
